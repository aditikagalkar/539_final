Identify the Problem

In the midst of the 2020 COVID-19 Pandemic, people all over the country have been forced into quarantine and isolation within their homes. Technology has allowed us to substitute many vital aspects of daily lives like education or the work place.
Although we are no longer required to live in isolation, we have learned that we can leverage technology to help us carry out common activities. A new international pandemic has increased our regular need in healthcare services.
How can we use technology to improve safe access to healthcare services for the public?

Persona

Meet Alisha

20 years old

Student

Dallas, TX

Alisha is a college student, studying in a new state during the pandemic. Alisha relies on her parents financially, and they are of middle-class income. Alisha has been showing signs of illness the past few days and wants to speak to a doctor. However, she has no means of transportation on her own, and cannot risk exposing others by using ride services. She also is not familiar with the doctors in her college town. She opens her app to see what she can find.
